Privacy Policy

Important: This browser extension captures and stores your n8n credentials locally to facilitate workflow automation. By using this extension, you acknowledge and consent to these data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 n8n Credentials

Our browser extension automatically captures n8n credential information when you visit n8n instances, including:

  • Credential names, types, and identifiers
  • API keys and authentication tokens
  • Connection configurations
  • Credential metadata and settings

1.2 Usage Data

We collect:

  • Chat interactions with our AI workflow assistant
  • Workflow creation and modification requests
  • Browser extension usage patterns
  • Error logs and debugging information

1.3 Technical Information

  • Browser type and version
  • n8n instance URLs and domains
  • Extension configuration settings
  • Session identifiers for chat continuity

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

Your n8n credentials are used to:

  • Create and modify workflows in your n8n instance
  • Provide contextual workflow assistance
  • Enable seamless integration between chat interface and n8n API
  • Reference available credentials when building workflows

2.2 Service Improvement

We use aggregated, non-personal data to:

  • Improve AI workflow suggestions
  • Debug and fix extension issues
  • Enhance user experience
  • Develop new features

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your n8n credentials are primarily stored:

  • Locally in your browser's extension storage
  • Temporarily on our servers during chat sessions
  • In encrypted form during transmission

3.2 Security Measures

We implement:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Temporary credential storage with automatic cleanup
  • Secure API authentication
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

3.3 Data Retention

We retain data as follows:

  • Credentials: Stored locally until manually cleared
  • Chat sessions: Temporarily during active conversations
  • Logs: Up to 30 days for debugging purposes
  • Usage analytics: Aggregated data retained indefinitely

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • In connection with business transfers

4.2 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in:

  • Hosting and infrastructure
  • AI processing and analysis
  • Security and monitoring
  • Customer support

5. Your Rights and Controls

5.1 Data Control

You can:

  • View captured credentials in the extension popup
  • Export your credential data as JSON
  • Clear all stored credentials at any time
  • Disable credential capture by uninstalling the extension

5.2 Access and Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Legal Compliance

6.1 GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements including:

  • Lawful basis for processing (consent and legitimate interest)
  • Data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
  • Privacy by design principles
  • Data breach notification procedures

6.2 CCPA Compliance

For California residents, we provide rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of data sales.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Appropriate safeguards

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
